Kynuna to Mt Isa - via Cloncurry...

Another day on road mainly just driving from point A to B. Luckily between Kynuna and Mt Isa is Cloncurry. Built amongst craggy mountains which provide spectacular scenery.

The wet season has left the normally barren hills green.

After a quick check on the local bakery, we headed to Chinaman Creek Dam.

A surprisingly picturesque spot...

The local council have provided a great recreational area and we are almost the only vistiors. A giant Wedge-tail Eagle sculpture keeps watch.

There was even time before lunch to do some birding...
