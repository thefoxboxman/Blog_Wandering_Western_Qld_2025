Kynuna - Home of Waltzing Matilda

Longreach to Kynuna today.

The roads are straight and flat, out across the plains. After a great wet season, the pastures are a sea of green grass as far as the eye can see.

The creeks and waterholes are full...

The cattle trucks keep on coming...

"Tumbleweed" piling up against the fence. Grass seed heads really...

Half way between Winton and Kynuna, out of the seeming endless plains jutt these interesting hills called the Pinicales. Time to crack out the drone...

Just before we reach Kynuna, we go to visit the Combo Water Hole, the scene of Banjo Patterson's ballad "Waltzing Matilda". We soon realise that the fly-nets are required...

Kynuna was home to huge flocks of Woodswallows...
