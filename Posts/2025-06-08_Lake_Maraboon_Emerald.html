Lake Maraboon - Emerald

Two nights at Lake Maraboon to catch up on shopping, washing and photo wrangling.

Jenelle spent the late afternoons each day at the lake shore photographing the many species of water birds.

We put the drone up to photograph an aerial view of the Faribairn Dam and Lake Maraboon, Emerald. The water level was well down despite the wet summer.
